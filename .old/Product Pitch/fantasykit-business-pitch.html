// The Market Gap

WHY CURRENT
FANTASY PRODUCTS FAIL

Fantasy football has massive reach but weak retention. FPL has 11M users but 60% churn after month one. The reason is simple: current products are built for solo play, not social rivalry.

🎯 Our Core Insight

Fantasy football is won or lost before kick-off — in the group chat. The banter, the trades, the trash talk after a rival's captain scores. That's where engagement lives. Current products treat social as an afterthought (add a chat tab, ship it). We're building social-first: every feature exists to create a reason for two users to interact.

📱

Existing Products

Solo optimization → check score → leave. Global leaderboards with strangers. No memory, no rivalry, no reason to return beyond the next match.

💬

The WhatsApp Reality

Users already live in group chats. They screenshot FPL scores and send them manually. The product doesn't facilitate banter — users do it despite the product.

🎯

Our Approach

Small private leagues (4–10 friends). Auto-generated call-out cards when rivals' captains score. Live rank changes. Shareable matchday recaps. The social layer is the product.

// Product Strategy

THREE CORE
RETENTION LOOPS

Every session maps to one of these loops. Built in this order — pre-match is the foundation, live is the hook, post-match is the social glue that converts one match into the next.

🎯

Pre-Match Decisions

Where strategy lives. Squad building, captain picks, injury alerts, predicted lineups, daily top-scorer predictions. The more meaningful a decision feels before kickoff, the more likely users open the app again.

⚡

Live Match Engagement

Real-time fantasy consequence. Users see points change the second a goal goes in. Live projections update every 5 minutes. Rival captain alerts. The app stays open for every minute of play.

🏆

Post-Match Resolution

Rankings settle, matchday recaps generate, shareable cards go to WhatsApp. This is where "one match" converts into "next match" through awards, recap shares, and league banter.

// Revenue Model

FOUR REVENUE
STREAMS

Monetization starts from partnership, not paywalls. We don't compete with existing live-score apps — we enhance them with the social layer they're missing.

🤝

Strategic Partnership

Co-market with established live-score platforms. They bring the match data and brand trust. We bring the social engagement layer. Revenue share on premium subscriptions and in-app purchases generated through our social features.

🎨

Cosmetic League Themes

Users unlock visual themes for their private leagues: alternative pitch colors, badge frames, card styles. First theme free after winning a matchday. Additional themes €1.99 one-time. No pay-to-win — pure status signaling within friend groups.

🏆

Sponsor-Backed Prizes

Partner with sports brands (kit, boots, betting-adjacent) to offer real prizes for tournament winners. Brand pays for reach, users get tangible rewards, we take a platform fee. Zero capital outlay for prize pools.

📊

Premium Analytics

Advanced stats dashboard, H2H detailed history, season archives, projection accuracy tracking. Free tier covers casual users, premium tier (€4.99/month) for competitive leagues who want the full intelligence layer.

🎯 Why Partnership-First Works

Live-score apps have users but weak engagement (open → check score → close). We have the engagement layer but need the distribution. A co-branded experience (e.g., "[Platform Name] Fantasy Leagues powered by FantasyKit") gives both sides what they're missing. They handle data feeds and brand, we handle retention and social virality. Win-win.

Live Projections

✅ Live

Projected Final Score

  • Formula: `current_pts + Σ(avg_per_90 × remaining_min/90)` per active player.
  • Position averages: GK 2.1 / DEF 2.8 / MID 3.2 / FWD 4.1 pts/90.
  • Updates every 5 minutes. Conservative: always floors, never overstates.
  • UI: trend arrows (↑ green / ↓ red / — grey), two-tone progress bar.
  • Rival Watch: each rival shows `+N live → N proj.` sub-label.
  • Engine: `lib/projections.js` — framework-agnostic.

// Future Expansion

THE THREE-YEAR
ROADMAP

Starting with World Cup 2026, we expand horizontally (more competitions) and vertically (deeper social features). The product is a platform, not a tournament-specific app.

🎯 Platform, Not App

FantasyKit isn't "World Cup Fantasy" — it's a fantasy football engine that works for any tournament or league. World Cup 2026 is the launch vehicle because it's global, high-engagement, and time-bound. But the same social mechanics apply to Premier League, La Liga, Serie A, and beyond. The roadmap is about unlocking those competitions progressively.

// Q3 2026 — Post World Cup

PHASE 1:
DOMESTIC LEAGUES

Immediately after World Cup 2026 ends, launch support for top domestic leagues. Users who loved the World Cup social experience can roll directly into their domestic league with the same friend group.

Target Leagues (Launch Order)
🏴󠁧󠁢󠁥󠁮󠁧󠁿

Premier League (England)

Highest global viewership. 38 matchdays, August–May. Partner with existing EPL data providers for live fixture feeds. Same social mechanics as World Cup, just longer season.

🇪🇸

La Liga (Spain)

Strong engagement in Latin America and Spain. 38 matchdays. Multi-competition leagues unlock here: users can create leagues that mix La Liga + Premier League for maximum flexibility.

🇵🇹

Primeira Liga (Portugal)

Home market advantage. 34 matchdays. Lower competition from existing products. Great test market for localized monetization (Portuguese sports brands for prizes).

🇮🇹

Serie A (Italy)

Strong tactical culture, high engagement. 38 matchdays. Unlock after La Liga to validate multi-league mechanics work at scale.

🔑 Multi-Competition Leagues

The key innovation: users can create private leagues that span multiple competitions. Want to track your friends across both La Liga and Premier League in one leaderboard? Done. This is impossible in FPL (Premier League only) or LaLiga Fantasy (La Liga only). We're the only product that lets you mix competitions in a single social league.

// 2027 — Beyond Football

PHASE 2:
CROSS-SPORT EXPANSION

Once the football social layer is proven and scaled, the same mechanics work for basketball, American football, cricket, rugby. The product becomes a multi-sport social fantasy platform.

Target Sports (Exploration Phase)
🏀

NBA (Basketball)

Daily fantasy already massive in US. Our angle: bring the social layer. Private leagues for friend groups watching games together. Live projections during 4th quarter. Shareable game recaps.

🏈

NFL (American Football)

Weekly matchday cycle (16 games/week). Perfect for our social mechanics. H2H rivalries over a 17-week season. Wildcard and Triple Captain chips adapt naturally to NFL scoring.

🏏

Cricket (IPL, T20 World Cup)

Massive in India, Pakistan, Australia. Short-format tournaments (IPL = 2 months) fit our model perfectly. Partner with Indian live-score apps for distribution.

🏉

Rugby (Six Nations, World Cup)

Strong engagement in UK, France, NZ, SA. Tournament-based (Six Nations = 5 matchdays). Low competition in fantasy rugby space.

🎯 Why Cross-Sport Works

The social mechanics (live projections, H2H records, shareable recaps, auto-generated banter cards) are sport-agnostic. They work for any game with: (a) discrete scoring events (goals, touchdowns, wickets), (b) a roster of players, (c) a scoring system. Football is the wedge. Multi-sport is the long-term platform play.

// 2027+ — Platform Maturity

ADVANCED
FEATURES

Features that deepen engagement once the core loops are validated at scale. These are not launch-critical but become differentiators as the product matures.

Intelligence Layer
🤖

AI-Powered Squad Suggestions

Machine learning model trained on historical performance + fixture difficulty + form. Suggests optimal squad picks before lock. Premium feature (€4.99/month). Increases win rate for casual users, keeps them engaged longer.

📈

Projection Accuracy Tracking

Show users how accurate our live projections were vs. actual final scores over the season. "Our projections were within ±2 pts 89% of the time." Builds trust in the intelligence layer.

Social Depth
🎙️

Voice Notes in League Chat

WhatsApp-style voice messages. Post-match rants, captain pick explanations, trash talk. Deepens the social layer beyond text. iOS/Android native integrations.

🏆

Season-Long Narrative Arcs

Auto-generated season recaps showing: biggest comebacks, longest win streaks, most heartbreaking losses. Shareable end-of-season video summary (think Spotify Wrapped for fantasy football).

👥

League Mergers & Expansion

Allow two private leagues to merge mid-season (playoff format). Or expand league size from 8 → 12 members. Unlocks network effects: users invite more friends to join ongoing leagues.

Monetization Evolution
💎

NFT Trophy Shelf

Season winners mint a digital trophy NFT as proof of victory. Trophy is shareable, tradable, collectible. Users who win multiple seasons build a verified trophy collection. Monetize through initial minting fees (€9.99/trophy).

🎫

Live Event Ticket Partnerships

Partner with stadiums/clubs to offer match-day experiences as prizes. Top scorer in a league wins 2 tickets to the next El Clásico. Brand partnerships (Adidas, Nike) sponsor the prizes, we take a platform fee.
