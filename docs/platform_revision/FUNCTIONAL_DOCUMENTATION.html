Classic Mode

Any manager can own any player simultaneously. No draft — buy freely from the open market within budget and position limits.

  • Player Repeats: Allowed across all managers
  • Market: Full open pool always visible
  • Transfers: 3 per matchday (buy or sell)
  • Best for: Casual leagues, season formats

Draft Mode

Each player belongs to exactly one manager. Snake-draft lottery before first matchday. Market only shows unallocated players.

  • Player Repeats: Forbidden (auto-relaxes in cup knockouts)
  • Draft: 45-slot ranked wish-list → commissioner lottery
  • Transfers: FCFS from unallocated pool only
  • Best for: Competitive leagues, cup formats

Draft System — 4-Phase Flow

1

Wish List

Rank up to 45 players in priority order. No constraints at submission — hedging allowed. Revisable until commissioner fires the lottery.

2

Snake Lottery

Random pick order → direction reverses every round. Player awarded if: available, within position cap, budget, and club cap. Skipped if wish list exhausted.

3

Squad Recovery

Incomplete squads enter FCFS market. initial_build_complete flag flips true at 15 players — enforcing normal transfer limits thereafter.

4

Cup Keep Window

Before knockout draft, managers protect up to 5 players. Pre-allocated in Pass 0 before snake runs.

Auto-Relaxation Engine

Club cap auto-expands as clubs are eliminated: 3→4→5→unlimited. Player-repeat unlocks in tiers as pool pressure rises. Zero commissioner effort.

⚡ QF Captain ×2 Multiplier

After QF draw is set, admin opens a 48h window. Pick one surviving roster player as captain. Reaches QF or beyond → total tournament score ×2. Eliminated before QF = no bonus. Mid-season decision point that revives interest in every tournament.

Masters Drop Rule — Built-in Forgiveness

Season total = all 4 Grand Slams + best 4 of 9 Masters + ATP Finals. Once ≥5 Masters complete, the 5 lowest scores auto-drop. Computed in get_player_box_leaderboard RPC. No user action needed. Creates late-season competitiveness.

One Login Per Tournament

Set roster once before draw — earns points passively as players advance. Lowest-friction fantasy format for casual players. App re-engagement driven by Clubhouse + notifications, not daily decisions.

04 · Tennis Ace Cards & ATP Finals Slate Power-ups + season finale prediction

Ace Cards — 4 per user per season, one per tournament (not available at ATP Finals)

Ace Card · Risk Play

Underdog Boost

Doubles all points earned by your 2 Tier 4 (unseeded) picks for the entire tournament. High-risk upside for backing unknowns.

↑ Tier 4 pts ×2 · tournament-wide

Ace Card · Insurance

Safety Net

Earns +200 flat bonus if your Tier 1 pick is eliminated in the 1st or 2nd main-draw match. Turns early upsets into silver linings.

↑ +200 pts if Tier 1 exits early

Ace Card · Power Play

Surface Specialist

Doubles total points earned by ALL 7 players in your roster for the entire tournament. The highest-value card — use wisely.

↑ ALL 7 players ×2 · tournament-wide

Ace Card · Accumulator

Dark Horse Insurance

+50 bonus for every round your Tier 4 players advance past the Round of 32. Rewards deep unseeded runs.

↑ +50 per Tier 4 per round past R32

Legal Design: Non-Withdrawable Closed Economy

Real money → coins via Stripe. Coins never convert back to cash. CHECK constraints enforce the invariant at DB layer (not just app logic). Platform stays in "game ledger" territory, not regulated gambling.

Proposition Types

TypeResolution Source
GW TotalBoth managers' full matchday fantasy points
Player vs PlayerSpecific player fantasy pts vs. another player
Match Result PickBoth pick a fixture result — correct wins

Rake Formula

rake = round(2 × stake × rate). Winner gets 2 × stake − rake. Rake burns via a commission ledger entry — no house wallet, pure deflationary economy accelerating re-purchase.

Challenge Lifecycle

1

Create

Select proposition + stake. Stake locked from balance to escrow (stake_lock transaction).

2

Accept / Decline

Opponent accepts (their stake locks) or declines (challenger refunded immediately, no rake).

3

Auto-Resolution

Cron every 15 min. Resolves only after roundComplete = true. Idempotent — safe to re-run.

Security Architecture

  • Server-authoritative: All coin moves via SECURITY DEFINER RPCs
  • FOR UPDATE locks: Deterministic order, prevents deadlocks
  • ALREADY_RESOLVED guard: Idempotent — resolves exactly once
  • Stripe HMAC-SHA256: Webhook signature verification
  • Nightly reconciliation: wallet.balance = SUM(transactions)
  • Daily spend cap: Configurable responsible-play limit per user

Architecture Principles

Server-authoritative: All value mutations in PostgreSQL RPCs — clients cannot tamper. Idempotent pipelines: Any job can re-run safely. Append-only audit trail: squad_events, coin_transactions, round_backups — disputes resolvable from history. Config-driven: New competition = DB insert, not a deployment.
