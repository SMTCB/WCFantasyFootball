────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
THE SITUATION IN ONE PARAGRAPH
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

FantasyKit is a fantasy football platform repositioning as a white-label SaaS.
The old "Forza" dark aesthetic is being retired. A new light direction ("Kit")
has been chosen. The full platform has already been designed in dark and handed
off to engineering — those specs exist as HTML prototypes in this project. The
light direction has been decided and proven at concept level but NOT yet built
as full dev-ready screens. The next job is to build those Kit (Light) screens.


────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
WHAT THE LIVE APP LOOKS LIKE TODAY
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

URL:       https://wc-fantasy-football.vercel.app
Email:     demo@fantasykit.test
Password:  ForzaDemo2026!

Dark near-black surfaces (#080A0E), cyan accent (#00B4D8), all-caps JetBrains
Mono everywhere, Archivo Black for display. The app has 5 main nav sections:
Scores · League · Squad · Live · Market.

The original Platform Assessment (redesign/Platform Assessment.html) documented
6 critical issues with this direction. Those are considered fixed by switching
to Kit (Light). Do not repeat them — they are already addressed.


────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
THE KIT (LIGHT) DESIGN TOKENS — SOURCE OF TRUTH
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

These are the exact values to use. Do not invent new colours.

  Surface
    Background:       #F7F3ED   (warm off-white, the base layer)
    Card:             #FFFFFF   (white cards on top of background)
    Raised surface:   #EDEAE2   (slightly darker than background, for panels)
    Shell / sidebar:  #18202E   (dark navy — the ONE dark element in the light theme)

  Text
    Primary:          #18202E   (same as shell — dark navy)
    Secondary:        #4B5568   (medium grey)
    Muted / labels:   #8A97A8   (light grey, for metadata)
    Rule / border:    #E2DDD5   (warm light grey for dividers)

  Accent
    Primary accent:   #1A6FA8   (blue — buttons, active states, lead numbers)
    Accent bg:        rgba(26,111,168,.08)   (very light blue tint for highlights)
    Gold:             #B8720E   (for captain, warnings, deadline urgency)
    Positive:         #166534   (dark green for good deltas, wins)
    Danger:           #B91C1C   (dark red for negative deltas, losses)

  Shape
    Border radius:    6px
    Capitalisation:   Mixed case (NOT all-caps like the old dark direction)
    Mono font usage:  Sparingly — labels and metadata only, not for all data

  Typography (unchanged from dark direction)
    Headlines / names: Archivo Black
    Body / UI:         Archivo 400 / 500 / 600
    Data / labels:     JetBrains Mono (used sparingly)

  White-label principle:
    A client re-skin requires changing only 3 values:
      --brand-accent  (their primary colour)
      --brand-name    (their logo/wordmark)
      their data      (league name, team names)
    Everything else stays constant.

WHAT HAS BEEN BUILT (KIT LIGHT DIRECTION — CONCEPT LEVEL)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  redesign/Identity Exploration.html
    A design canvas (pan/zoom) showing the Kit direction at concept level.
    Sections: Visual Foundation · Key Screens · Mobile · White Label.
    Contains: colour/type/component swatches for both directions,
    a League Hub leaderboard screen, a Squad pitch (dark only), mobile
    League Hub, and a La Liga Fantasy brand-swap demo.
    Open in browser. Use arrow keys / scroll to navigate artboards.
    NOTE: requires redesign/design-canvas.jsx to be in the same folder (it is).

  redesign/FantasyKit UX Analysis.html  (+ redesign/assets/kit.css)
    A long-form analysis document identifying 6 NEW UX/UI improvements
    BEYOND the original 6 findings. Built in the Kit (Light) aesthetic.
    Each finding has: before (dark) / after (light) mockups, rationale,
    and a recommendation. Finding 01 has 3 directional options.
    Also contains: white-label brand swap demo, phased roadmap.

    The 6 new improvements are:
      01 · No clear next action (deadline-driven primary CTA banner)
      02 · No onboarding (first-run checklist + empty-state coaching)
      03 · Flat visual hierarchy (three type roles: lead / support / label)
      04 · Density without focus (player drawer / progressive disclosure)
      05 · Silent system (action feedback: toasts, undo, impact preview)
      06 · Mobile = shrunk desktop (card patterns, thumb-reach FAB)

  redesign/Platform Assessment.html
    The original audit document (6 findings, 3 directions considered).
    Read this for context on WHY the direction changed. Do not repeat
    its findings in new work — they are already addressed by Kit Light.

WHAT NEEDS TO BE BUILT NEXT (KIT LIGHT FULL HANDOFFS)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

None of the design_handoff_* folders have been ported to Kit Light yet.
The following screens need to be built as full hi-fi Kit Light prototypes,
incorporating BOTH the existing dark screen structure AND the 6 new UX fixes.

Priority order (from the UX Analysis roadmap):

  PHASE 1 — Build first (low effort, highest impact)
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────
  1. League Hub — Leaderboard tab
     The most-visited screen. Must include:
     - Kit Light tokens throughout
     - Dark sidebar (--shell: #18202E) + warm white content area
     - Deadline action banner (Finding 01): countdown + "Set your squad" + one CTA
     - Three-tier type hierarchy on standings rows (Finding 03):
         lead number = accent Archivo Black, name = Archivo Black, GW = mono small
     - Tab-only nav (no competing button row)
     - Right panel: deadline card, recent activity, my squad card
     - Desktop (1280px) + Mobile (390px) as cards + FAB
     Reference the dark version in design_handoff_league_hub/ for structure.

  2. Player Market
     Must include:
     - Kit Light tokens
     - Clean scannable rows: position chip, name, form, price (Finding 04)
     - Player drawer on tap: full stats + action + impact preview (Findings 04+05)
     - Action feedback: toast + undo after signing (Finding 05)
     Reference: FORZAKIT Final.html (dark market) for structure.

  3. Action feedback patterns (system-wide)
     - Toast component (Finding 05)
     - Points-impact preview chip (Finding 05)
     - Squad locked / saved state markers
     These appear across League Hub, Market, and Squad.

  PHASE 2 — Build next (medium effort)
  ─────────────────────────────────────
  4. My Squad — pitch view in Kit Light
     This is a different design problem from the dark pitch. The pitch surface
     needs to work on a light background (not just invert — redesign the field).
     Player tokens adapt to light. Captain marker still gold.
     Reference: FORZAKIT Final.html dark pitch + Identity Exploration concept.

  5. Mobile patterns (across all screens)
     - Card-based leaderboard (Finding 06)
     - Bottom tab bar for product nav
     - Strip tabs inside League Hub
     - Thumb-reachable FAB for primary action
     Reference: design_handoff_league_hub/Leagues Mobile.html (dark) for structure.

  PHASE 3 — Launch prep
  ──────────────────────
  6. Onboarding / First-run experience (Finding 02)
     - 3-step checklist: join league → pick 15 → choose captain
     - Progress tracking
     - Empty-state coaching (pitch, leaderboard, market)
     - Auto-pick escape hatch
     This screen does NOT exist in any dark handoff — it is net new.

  7. Remaining League Hub tabs in Kit Light
     - Frontpage (Forza Times newspaper — keep the cream paper, update chrome)
     - Bets & Predictions
     - Auctions
     - Chat
     - Stats
     Reference: design_handoff_league_hub/ for full structure.

  8. Player Modal in Kit Light
     Reference: design_handoff_player_modals/ for structure.

  9. Live Centre in Kit Light
     Reference: design_handoff_live_centre/ for structure.

  10. White-label validation
      Apply a client brand (e.g. La Liga red #CF1732) across one full screen
      to confirm the 3-token swap works end-to-end.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
IMPORTANT DECISIONS ALREADY MADE (DO NOT REVISIT)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

- Kit (Light) is the chosen direction. Stadium (Dark) becomes dark mode only.
- "FantasyKit" name and Forza brand are retired. Wordmark is a placeholder.
- Archivo Black + Archivo + JetBrains Mono type pairing is kept (unchanged).
- Mono (JetBrains Mono) is used sparingly — labels only, not for all data.
- Border radius is 6px (not the old sharp 2-4px).
- Mixed case, not all-caps, throughout (except label eyebrows).
- White-label means 3-token swap: --brand-accent, --brand-name, their data.
- Navigation: left sidebar (desktop) + bottom tab bar (mobile). No dual CTA bar.
- Inside League: 5 tabs max (Leaderboard, Feed, Bets, Squad, Market). Not 8.
- The Forza Times (Frontpage) newspaper keeps its cream paper inside the
  light chrome — it remains the brand showcase moment.
